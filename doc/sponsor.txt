DirectoryStorage Sponsorship
============================

DirectoryStorage development has been sponsored by:

  * Toby Dickenson

  * `Gemini Data Loggers`_ - robustness testing and replication.

  * Mediatria s.r.l., Milano (I) `www.altoforno.net`_ - the `ds2fs`_
    tool for converting DirectoryStorage to FileStorage.

  * `Max Planck Institut fuer Kognitions- und Neurowissenschaften`_ -
    Zope 3 support and other contributions by Philipp von
    Weitershausen

There are currently no sponsors of further DirectoryStorage
maintenance, development, and support.  If you find DirectoryStorage
valuable, or the free technical support to be useful, then you are
encouraged to consider `supporting this project`_ through donations.
